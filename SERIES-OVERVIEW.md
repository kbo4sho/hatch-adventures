## Story Structure (Template)

**ACT 1: THE EXIT (10%)**
- Kids exit a hatch into the new setting
- First impressionsâ€”what do they see/hear/smell?
- Mia's innocent observation sets the tone

**ACT 2: THE DISCOVERY (30%)**
- They explore and meet someone/something
- Max asks questions, Mia finds something fun
- A small problem or mystery appears

**ACT 3: THE ADVENTURE (40%)**
- They help solve the problem
- Teamwork moment (Max's knowledge + Mia's creativity)
- Exciting/funny/heartwarming climax

**ACT 4: THE ENTRANCE (20%)**
- Resolution and lesson learned
- They find the next hatch
- Hint/tease about where it might lead
- Enter the hatch together

## Target Formats
- Written stories (500-800 words, ages 3-7)

## Tone
- Warm, optimistic, playful
- No scary/dark themes
- Celebrate curiosity, kindness, teamwork
- Diverse settings (history, space, nature, fantasy, everyday magic)

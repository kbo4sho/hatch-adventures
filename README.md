## ğŸ“– Story Formula (What Makes Them Work)

### The Characters
- **Max (7):** Curious, protective, uses knowledge to solve problems
- **Mia (3):** Fearless, creative, finds joy in simple things
- **Together:** Teamwork, kindness, learning through adventure

### The Structure
- **Hatch = Portal:** Each story is a self-contained adventure
- **Start & End:** Always begins exiting one hatch, ends entering another
- **Cliffhanger Lite:** Tease the next setting to keep kids excited
- **Age-Appropriate:** Simple language, short sentences, positive themes

### The Lessons
Every story teaches something:
- Kindness, sharing, teamwork
- Curiosity, bravery, creativity
- Celebrating differences
- Nature, science, emotions

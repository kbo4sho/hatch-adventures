## 01.png — Finding the Hatch

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. A sunny backyard with a big old oak tree. A 7-year-old boy with brown hair and a blue t-shirt (Max) is on his hands and knees near the tree roots, reaching for a stuffed white bunny. His 3-year-old sister with blonde pigtails and a pink dress (Mia) stands behind him pointing down excitedly. Between the roots, a round metal hatch is visible in the ground, covered in swirling blue glowing patterns. The handle is shaped like a star. Soft blue light spills out from the hatch edges. Green grass, wooden fence, warm afternoon sunlight.

## 02.png — Down the Golden Tunnel

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. Two kids (a boy in a blue shirt and a girl in a pink dress with blonde pigtails) sliding together down a swirling magical tunnel filled with warm golden light. They're holding hands, laughing, hair flying. The tunnel curves like a waterslide with glowing golden walls and floating sparkles. Motion blur suggests speed. Whimsical, exciting, zero fear — pure joy. Vibrant saturated colors.

## 03.png — The Dinosaur Garden

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. Two small children standing on soft green moss, mouths open in wonder, looking out at an enormous lush prehistoric garden. Massive trees tower overhead against a bright orange sky. Giant ferns sway gently. Flowers as big as beach balls bloom in vivid reds, purples, and yellows. Behind the children, a glowing blue hatch is embedded in a mossy hillside. In the distance between the trees, the silhouette of a long-necked dinosaur is visible. Sense of awe and discovery. Rich colors, magical atmosphere.

## 04.png — Meeting the Brachiosaurus

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. A huge friendly Brachiosaurus bending her long neck down to greet two small children in a prehistoric garden. She has warm brown eyes and a gentle smile. The boy (brown hair, blue shirt) looks up in amazement. The little girl (blonde pigtails, pink dress) waves happily and says hi. Giant ferns and colorful flowers frame the scene. Bright orange sky. The dinosaur is enormous but clearly gentle — like a friendly giant. Warm golden light. Safe and magical.

## 05.png — The Dinosaur Picnic

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. A joyful outdoor picnic scene in a prehistoric garden. Several friendly dinosaurs sit around flat rocks covered with piles of purple berries, green leaves, and giant colorful fruits. A Triceratops munches flowers. A Stegosaurus swishes its spiky tail nearby. Three small baby dinosaurs chase each other playfully. The Brachiosaurus gently nudges a pile of purple berries toward two children sitting on the moss. The boy takes a berry and smiles. The girl giggles as a baby dinosaur sniffs her hand. Peaceful and communal. Bright and colorful.

## 06.png — Playing with the Dinosaurs

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. A playful scene in a prehistoric garden. A boy (blue shirt) gives a happy Triceratops a belly rub — the Triceratops is on its side with a blissful expression, legs in the air. Nearby, a small girl (pink dress, blonde pigtails) laughs as a baby dinosaur licks her hand. Two more baby dinosaurs peek out from behind giant ferns, playing hide and seek. In the background, a Stegosaurus hums a song (small musical notes float around it). Bright, full of life and laughter.

## 07.png — The Waterfall Goodbye

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. A beautiful cascading waterfall in a prehistoric garden at dusk. The orange sky is turning purple. The friendly Brachiosaurus gently places a small smooth glowing stone into a boy's outstretched hands — the stone glows with the same blue swirling patterns as the hatches. Behind the waterfall, a glowing hatch is visible in the rock face. The little girl (blonde pigtails) hugs a baby dinosaur goodbye. Other dinosaurs watch from nearby. Bittersweet but warm farewell. Rainbow light in the waterfall mist. Emotional and beautiful.

## 08.png — The Pink Tunnel Awaits

Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting. Two children standing at the open waterfall hatch, peering into a new tunnel that glows soft pink with floating sparkles and twinkling lights. The boy holds the girl's hand and a stuffed white bunny. Both look excited and curious — ready for the next adventure. Behind them through the waterfall mist, friendly dinosaurs wave goodbye. The contrast of warm pink tunnel light against the blue-green prehistoric garden. Sense of wonder and anticipation. Magical and hopeful.

## Character Reference

- **Max:** 7 years old, brown hair, blue t-shirt, khaki shorts, curious and brave
- **Mia:** 3 years old, blonde pigtails, pink dress, joyful and fearless
- **Flop:** Small white stuffed bunny, floppy ears
- **Brachiosaurus:** Warm earth-tone skin, kind brown eyes, gentle giant
- **Baby dinosaurs:** Small, playful, puppy-like energy

## Style Guide

- **Every prompt starts with:** `Children's book illustration style, warm and inviting.`
- Vibrant, saturated colors — never dark or scary
- Warm golden lighting throughout
- Friendly, approachable dinosaurs (not realistic/threatening)
- Consistent character designs across all 8 images
- Safe, magical, wonder-filled atmosphere

## Strategy: Full Story vs. Clips

### Full Story (3-5 minutes)
- Upload complete story to TikTok (10 min max)
- Upload to Instagram Reels (90 sec max) in parts
- Best for: Building loyal audience

### Short Clips (15-60 seconds)
- Teaser: First scene + "Watch full story..."
- Highlight: Best moment from story
- Behind-the-scenes: Character art, making-of
- Best for: Discovery & virality

**Recommendation:** Do BOTH! Post clips throughout the week + full story on weekends.

**TikTok:**
- Views (goal: 1,000+ first week)

- Shares (best indicator of value)
- Profile visits ‚Üí clicks to bio link

**Instagram:**

**Engagement:**
- Reply to EVERY comment first day

- Duet/stitch trending sounds (TikTok)

**Collaboration:**
- Tag: @preschoolteachers, @kidlit, @momlife accounts

- Cross-promote: Mention Spotify in TikTok, etc.

**Paid Ads (Optional):**

## Legal/Safety Notes

### Music & Copyright
- **Safe:** Use original audio from your videos
- **Risky:** Popular music (can get muted/removed)
- **Best:** Royalty-free music from:
  - Instagram's music library
  - Epidemic Sound
  - Artlist
  - Uppbeat

### Kids' Privacy (COPPA)
- ‚úÖ DO: Show your own kids (with permission)
- ‚ùå DON'T: Show other people's kids without release
- ‚úÖ DO: Encourage fan art
- ‚ùå DON'T: Repost kids' faces without parent permission

### Comments
- Monitor closely
- Delete spam/inappropriate
- Highlight positive comments
- Consider limiting comments if needed

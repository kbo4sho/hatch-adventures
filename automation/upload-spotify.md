## One-Time Setup (10 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Podcast on Anchor

1. Go to https://anchor.fm
2. Sign up (free account)
3. Click "Create a podcast"
4. Fill in details:
   - **Name:** "The Hatch Adventures"
   - **Description:** "Magical story adventures for kids ages 3-7. Join Max and Mia as they discover hatches that take them to incredible places!"
   - **Category:** Kids & Family > Stories for Kids
   - **Language:** English
   - **Cover art:** Upload your series cover image (1400x1400px minimum)

### Step 2: Auto-Submit to Spotify

1. In Anchor dashboard, go to **Distribution**
2. Click **"Distribute to Spotify"**
3. Anchor automatically submits your podcast
4. Approval takes 1-3 days
5. You'll get an email when it's live

## Uploading Each Episode

### Method A: Via Anchor Website (Easiest)

1. Log into Anchor.fm
2. Click **"New episode"**
3. Upload your audio file:
   - File: `story-##-[name]-audio.mp3`
   - Title: "Episode #: [Story Title]"
   - Description: Copy from your story markdown file
4. Add episode art (optional - can reuse series cover)
5. Click **"Publish now"** or **"Schedule"**

**Done!** Anchor auto-distributes to Spotify + all other platforms.

---

### Method B: Via Anchor Mobile App (On the Go)

1. Open Anchor app on iPhone/Android
2. Tap **"+"** to create episode
3. Upload audio or record
4. Add title & description
5. Publish

---

### Method C: RSS Feed (Advanced)

If you want more control, use a different podcast host:

**Podcast Hosts (all support Spotify):**
- **Podbean** - Free tier, good analytics
- **Buzzsprout** - $12/month, best UI

**Then submit RSS feed to Spotify:**
1. Go to https://podcasters.spotify.com
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Submit your RSS feed URL
4. Wait for approval (1-3 days)

## Best Practices

### Audio Quality
- **Format:** MP3, 128kbps minimum (192kbps recommended)
- **Mono or Stereo:** Stereo sounds better
- **Peak levels:** -3dB to -1dB (not clipping)
- **Background music:** Optional, keep 20-30dB below voice

### Episode Length
- **Ideal:** 3-7 minutes (perfect for kids' attention span)
- **Max:** 15 minutes
- **Consistency:** Try to keep similar lengths

### Publishing Schedule
- **Weekly:** Best for building audience
- **Bi-weekly:** Good balance
- **Batch upload:** Upload 3-5 at launch, then weekly

### SEO (Discoverability)
- Use keywords in title: "Kids Story", "Bedtime Story", "Adventure"
- Include age range in description
- Tag relevant topics: dinosaurs, friendship, etc.
- Link to your social media in show notes

## Common Issues

**Q: Episode not showing up on Spotify?**
- Wait 24-48 hours after publishing
- Check Spotify for Podcasters dashboard
- Verify RSS feed is active

**Q: Audio quality sounds bad?**
- Re-export at 192kbps
- Check mic levels weren't clipping
- Remove background noise

**Q: Can I edit an episode after publishing?**
- Yes! Re-upload the audio file
- Changes reflect in 24 hours

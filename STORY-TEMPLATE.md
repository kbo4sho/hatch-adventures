## Writing Tips:
- Keep sentences short and simple
- Use lots of action words
- Include sounds (whoosh, thump, giggle)
- Let Mia ask funny/simple questions
- Let Max explain things simply
- Always end on a hopeful/exciting note
- Each story should stand alone but connect to the series

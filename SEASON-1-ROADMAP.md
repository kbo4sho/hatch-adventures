## Production Pipeline (Per Episode)

1. **Story** ‚Äî Write 650-800 word story with clear scenes
2. **Narration Script** ‚Äî Break story into intro + 8 scenes + outro
3. **Image Prompts** ‚Äî Generate 8 detailed scene prompts
4. **Images** ‚Äî Kevin creates via AI tool
5. **Title Cards** ‚Äî Auto-generate intro/outro cards (00.png, 09.png)
6. **Audio** ‚Äî OpenAI TTS narration (Nova voice)
7. **Video** ‚Äî Render 4 platform versions (YouTube, TikTok, IG Reel, IG Feed)
